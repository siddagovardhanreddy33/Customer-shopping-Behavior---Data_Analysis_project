# 🎯 📌 Problem Statement

**Project Title:**
**Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis for Revenue Optimization**

**Problem Statement:**

A retail company has collected customer transaction data but lacks actionable insights into purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and factors influencing sales. Without these insights, the company is unable to effectively optimize marketing strategies, improve customer experience, or maximize revenue.

This project aims to analyze customer shopping behavior to uncover key trends, identify high-value customers, evaluate the impact of discounts and promotions, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance.

---


# ❓ 📊 Key Questions to Answer

We’ll divide them like a real analyst would:

---

## 👥 1. Customer Segmentation (WHO)

* Which age group contributes the most to revenue?
* Do male or female customers spend more on average?
* Which locations generate the highest sales?
* Are certain demographics more likely to be repeat customers?

---

## 🛍️ 2. Product Analysis (WHAT)

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which items are most frequently purchased?
* Are certain sizes or colors more popular?
* Which categories have the highest customer ratings?

---

## 💰 3. Sales & Revenue Analysis (HOW MUCH)

* What is the total revenue generated?
* What is the average purchase amount per transaction?
* Who are the top spending customers?
* What is the distribution of purchase amounts?

---

## 📅 4. Time-Based Trends (WHEN)

* Which season has the highest sales?
* Are there seasonal preferences for specific categories?
* Does customer spending vary across seasons?

---

## 🎯 5. Discounts & Promotions (WHY THEY BUY)

* Do discounts increase purchase amount or just frequency?
* Are customers using promo codes spending more?
* How do subscribed vs non-subscribed customers behave?
* Which payment methods are most used?

---

## ⭐ 6. Customer Satisfaction (QUALITY)

* What factors influence review ratings?
* Do higher purchases lead to higher satisfaction?
* Which categories receive the best and worst ratings?

---

## 🔁 7. Customer Loyalty & Behavior (RETENTION)

* Do customers with more previous purchases spend more?
* How does purchase frequency relate to revenue?
* Are repeat customers more valuable than new customers?
